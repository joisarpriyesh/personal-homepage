Publications

Study of New Approach to Select Optimum Configuration for HAPS
Using Discrete-MCDM Methods

Springer Nature 2025

An objective approach using Discrete Multi-Criteria Decision Making (D-MCDM) is proposed to select optimal aircraft configurations at the conceptual stage. A HAPS case study demonstrates its effectiveness in identifying the best design based on aerodynamic performance.

Upcoming Papers

ETAAV 2025

A Physics-Based Framework for Selecting Optimal UAV Configuration

A physics-based framework is proposed for unbiased UAV configuration selection through comprehensive design space exploration. Aerodynamic and performance analyses identify the most efficient configuration, validated using RANS simulations.

Proceedings forthcoming in IEEE Xplore

A Multidisciplinary Analysis & Optimization (MDAO) Framework for Conceptual UAV Design

A  Multidisciplinary Analysis and Optimization (MDAO) framework is developed for UAV design at the conceptual stage, integrating aerodynamics, structures, and performance analyses. The framework, implemented using FlightStream and OpenMDAO, enables systematic and merit-based optimization.
